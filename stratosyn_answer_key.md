## Full Solution Summary

```
Given:    dV/dt = -2 ft³/min
Cone:     H = 12 ft, R = 4 ft
Find:     dh/dt when h = 6 ft

Step 1 — Similar triangles:
          R/H = 4/12 = 1/3  →  r = h/3

Step 2 — Volume formula (one variable):
          V = (1/3)π(h/3)²h = (π/27)h³

Step 3 — Differentiate with respect to t:
          dV/dt = (π/9)h²(dh/dt)

Step 4 — Substitute known values:
          -2 = (π/9)(36)(dh/dt)
          -2 = 4π(dh/dt)
          dh/dt = -1/(2π) ft/min ≈ -0.159 ft/min

Step 5 — Interpret:
          At h = 6 ft, the water level is FALLING
          at 1/(2π) feet per minute.
          Negative sign = decreasing depth.
```
